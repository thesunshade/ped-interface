Enter a word or part of word. Then click on an entry to see the definition.
Regex is allowed. For example:
  • ^dham will give results that start with "dham"
  • am$ will give results that end with "am"
  • dh*am will give results that contain either "dham" or "dam" with fuzzy mode disabled
  • su[pv]a will give results that contain either "supa" or "suva"
