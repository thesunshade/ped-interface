Use RegEx for wildcards. For example:
  • ^dham gives words that start with "dham"
  • ^...dhama gives words that start with any three characters followed by "dhama"
  • am$ gives words that end with "am"
  • ^abhi.*ika$ gives words that start with "abhi" and ends with "ika"
  • dh*am gives words that contain either "dham" or "dam"
  • su[pv]a gives words that contain either "supa" or "suva"

For best results, disable fuzzy mode when using wildcards.
